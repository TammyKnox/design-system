Usage Considerations

  • The navigation should be used on all pages.
  • The navigation should be at the top of all pages.
  • The logo should always be linked to the home page.

Accessibility Considerations

The navigation bar uses a dark background with bolded light text to meet the WCAG requirements. It's important to always use a very light text over the dark background.
